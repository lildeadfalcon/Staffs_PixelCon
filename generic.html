SpecialEffect

Helping people with disabilities to enjoy video games

About them

By using technology ranging from modified joypads to eye-control, they're finding ways for people to play to the very best of their abilities. They're not just doing it for fun. By levelling the playing field, they are bringing families and friends together and having a profoundly positive impact on confidence and rehabilitation. Everyone's abilities are different. So SpecialEffect visit people, find out what they want to play, and exactly what they need to play it. They then match, modify or create equipment to lend to them, and give support so the people they help can get the best out of it.

As a community, gamers have always had each others backs one way or another. granted there can be some salt every now and then but majoritly we are a unified group. This charity is helping more people, less fortunate than us, become apart of our cluster. Which is why we wanted to support them using this event. To raise as much money for the kind hearted in order to bring more gamers into the world.
