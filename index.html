A day of epic hunger games' is coming to Stoke-On-Trent
for charity on the 25th of April

A chance for the community to come together for a good cause and an epic brawl
If you can't make it to the event, catch it live on Twitch

  • Tickets

Teams

Each game will have six teams of two going head to head
If you're interested in competing, check out our sign up page

Location

The Event will be held in the LRV venue in Stoke on Trent on the Staffordshire University Campus.
Address: Leek Rd, Stoke-on-Trent ST4 2DF

Other games

We will be hosting other games and consoles for attendees to try out as well. These include a VR Headset on which guests will be able to try out VR minecraft.

Additional Information

Incase you need to know more that we haven't covered,
contact us via our email address: b019592i@student.staffs.ac.uk

  • Transport

    Stoke-on-Trent train station is nearby,and there are multiple car parks around the venue and the general stoke area.

  • food

    Either side of the venue there are food establishments to fill your hunger bar. The Union shop is good for snacks, or if you want tasty meals, the verve is the place to go.

  • The Game

    The games will be played on a custom map made for this event, on our own minecraft dedicated server hosted by Pebblehost.

  • Schedule

    The Event will be from 10am-5pm with 5 lots of 30 minute matches making up the tournament.
